🎒 What to Bring

  • Tent & sleeping gear
  • Flashlight/headlamp

  • Layers for cool nights

Dogs are welcome at camp! Please:

  • Keep dogs leashed
  • Clean up after them
  • Ensure they're friendly with other dogs
  • Bring their food, water bowl & bed
